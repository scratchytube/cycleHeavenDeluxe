About

Just two dudes from New York who share a love of motorcycles. Upon meeting at a coding bootcamp, Shevaughn Grant and Brian Sahota realized they had passions outside of coding and upon graduation they started a custom motorcycle shop. Their builds have been featured in motorcycle magazines and movies across the world. They even scored the contract to manufacture the BatBike for Batman. Cycle Heaven loves unique projects and are looking for new clients to work with currently. Drop us a line and set up an appointment or just say hi!
